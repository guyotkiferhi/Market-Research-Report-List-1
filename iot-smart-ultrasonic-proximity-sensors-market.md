<p><strong>The IoT Smart Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam</li><li>Retro-Reflective</li><li>Diffuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT Smart Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors market comprises three main types: Through-Beam, Retro-Reflective, and Diffuse sensors. Through-Beam sensors consist of separate transmitter and receiver units that provide accurate detection over long distances, ideal for open spaces. Retro-Reflective sensors use a reflector to bounce signals back to the sensor, working well in enclosed environments. Diffuse sensors emit ultrasonic waves that reflect off nearby objects, suitable for detecting proximity in varied applications. Each type serves unique operational needs within the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
