<p><p>The cementless knee system market is categorized into three main types: Femoral, Tibial, and Hip systems. The Femoral system involves components that provide stability and mobility at the knee joint, utilizing press-fit technology for secure implantation. The Tibial system focuses on the lower leg's support, enhancing load distribution while promoting bone integration. The Hip system addresses hip joint replacement, adopting a similar cementless approach to ensure long-term fixation and improved patient outcomes. Each system emphasizes biocompatibility and durability.</p></p>
