<p><p>The roofing membrane system market comprises three primary types: thermoplastic membranes, synthetic rubber (thermoset), and modified bitumen. Thermoplastic membranes, typically PVC or TPO, offer flexibility and ease of installation, while synthetic rubber membranes are characterized by their durability and resistance to extreme weather, making them a popular choice for commercial applications. Modified bitumen combines the advantages of asphalt and modified polymers, providing enhanced performance and adaptability. Overall, these systems cater to diverse roofing needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
