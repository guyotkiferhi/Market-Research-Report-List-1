<p><p>The Non-Contact Position Sensor market includes two main types: linear position sensors and rotary position sensors. Linear position sensors measure displacement along a straight line without physical contact, utilizing technologies such as laser or capacitive sensing. Rotary position sensors, on the other hand, detect angular position or rotation, commonly employing magnetic or optical methods. Both types are essential in various applications, including automation, robotics, and automotive systems, where reliability and durability are crucial for accurate position tracking.</p></p>
