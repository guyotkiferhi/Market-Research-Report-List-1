FISH (Fluorescent In-situ Hybridization) uses fluorescent probes to visualize specific genetic material, aiding in cancer and genetic disorder diagnosis. Spectral Karyotype Imaging allows for detailed chromosome analysis using multiple colors, enhancing cytogenetic studies. DNA Microarrays enable simultaneous analysis of thousands of genes, facilitating comprehensive genetic profiling and biomarker discovery, driving advancements in personalized medicine.</p></p>
