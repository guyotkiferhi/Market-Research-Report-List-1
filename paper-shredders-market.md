<p><ul><li>Strip-cut shredders</li><li>Cross-cut</li><li>Particle-cut</li><li>Cardboard shredders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper shredders market comprises several types, each serving different needs. Strip-cut shredders provide basic, linear cuts for less sensitive documents, while cross-cut shredders deliver a higher security level by cutting paper into smaller pieces. Particle-cut shredders offer maximum protection by grinding paper into tiny particles, ideal for highly confidential materials. Cardboard shredders are specialized for recycling cardboard, converting it into manageable pieces for disposal or repurposing. Each type caters to varying security requirements and material processing needs.</p></p>
