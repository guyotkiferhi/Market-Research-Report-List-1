<p><p>The Electric Bath Lift market consists of two primary types: bath-side lifts and lifts that operate within the bathtub. Bath-side lifts are positioned next to the tub, allowing users to be transferred safely in and out of the bath. They typically feature adjustable heights and easy controls. In contrast, lifts in the bathtub are designed to lower and raise users directly within the tub, providing a more intimate bathing experience and ensuring safety for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
