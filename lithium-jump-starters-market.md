<p><ul><li>Self-Contained</li><li>Plug-In Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lithium Jump Starters market is divided into two main types: self-contained units and plug-in units. Self-contained jump starters are portable devices with built-in batteries, allowing users to start their vehicles without needing external power sources. These units are convenient and easy to use, ideal for emergencies. Plug-in units, on the other hand, require a power source, typically from the vehicle's electrical system, to jump-start the battery. They are usually more powerful but less portable compared to self-contained jump starters.</p></p>
