<p><p>Quartz Microbalance (QMB) technology measures mass change with high sensitivity by detecting frequency shifts in quartz crystals. The market segments include gravimetric Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), which accurately quantifies mass changes due to adsorption or desorption processes, and non-gravimetric QCM, which focuses on applications such as film thickness measurement without directly measuring mass. Both types are essential in fields like material science, biosensing, and surface chemistry, enabling advancements in research and industrial processes by providing critical data on material properties.</p></p>
