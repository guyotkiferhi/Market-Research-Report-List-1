<p><p>The residential HVAC air filters market comprises various types, primarily including pleated and panel air filters. Pleated HVAC air filters feature a large surface area due to their folded design, enhancing dust and allergen capture while promoting airflow. They are generally more efficient and have a longer lifespan. In contrast, panel HVAC air filters are flat and simpler in design, providing basic filtration for larger particles. However, they may require more frequent replacement and offer lower filtration efficiency compared to pleated options.</p></p>
