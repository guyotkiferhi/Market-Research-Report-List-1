<p><ul><li>Internal Turning</li><li>External Turning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy turning tools market is divided into two primary types: internal turning tools and external turning tools. Internal turning tools are designed for machining the inner surfaces of cylindrical workpieces, enabling precise operations like boring and grooving. Conversely, external turning tools are used for shaping the outer surfaces, allowing for various processes such as turning, facing, and threading. Both types are essential in manufacturing industries, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in machining applications for heavy-duty materials.</p></p>
