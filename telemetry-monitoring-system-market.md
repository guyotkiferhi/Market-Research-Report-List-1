<p><p>The Telemetry Monitoring System market is categorized into two primary types: wireless and wired systems. Wireless telemetry utilizes radio signals or cellular networks to transmit data, offering flexibility and easy installation in remote or difficult-to-wire environments. In contrast, wired telemetry systems rely on physical cables to transfer data, providing stable connections and often superior security and reliability. Each type serves specific applications in industries like healthcare, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation, catering to diverse operational requirements and infrastructure limitations.</p></p>
