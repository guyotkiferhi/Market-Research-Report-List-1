<p><ul><li>Isotropic Rubber Magnets</li><li>Anisotropic Rubber Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible rubber magnets are categorized into two primary types: isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic rubber magnets possess uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making them versatile for various applications, such as signage and craft projects. In contrast, anisotropic rubber magnets exhibit stronger magnetic properties in a specific direction, providing enhanced performance for applications requiring higher strength, like industrial uses or specialized devices. Both types are widely utilized due to their flexibility, durability, and ease of use in diverse settings.</p></p>
