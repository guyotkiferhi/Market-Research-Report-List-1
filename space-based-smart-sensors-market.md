<p><p>Space-based smart sensors are categorized into two main types based on their resistance to radiation: radiation-hardened and radiation-tolerant. Radiation-hardened sensors are designed to withstand harsh space environments, ensuring reliability and longevity in mission-critical applications. In contrast, radiation-tolerant sensors can endure some radiation exposure but may experience performance degradation over time. Both types are essential for spacecraft and satellite systems, providing critical data while maintaining functionality in the challenging conditions of outer space.</p></p>
