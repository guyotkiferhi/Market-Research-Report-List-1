<p><p>The orthodontic consumables market encompasses essential components like ligatures, brackets, and archwires used in dental braces. Ligatures are small ties that secure archwires to brackets, ensuring proper tooth alignment. Brackets are attached to each tooth and serve as the anchor points for the archwires. Archwires are flexible wires that connect the brackets and apply pressure to move teeth into desired positions. Together, these components are crucial for effective orthodontic treatment, influencing patient comfort and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
