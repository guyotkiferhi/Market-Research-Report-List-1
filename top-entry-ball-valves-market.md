<p><p>Top entry ball valves are classified into three main types based on their actuation methods: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Electric-type valves utilize electric power for operation, offering precise control and automation capabilities. Pneumatic types are driven by compressed air, allowing for rapid actuation and simpler integration with remote systems. Hydraulic types operate using pressurized fluid, providing high force and control in heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific industry needs, emphasizing efficiency, reliability, and automation in fluid management systems.</p></p>
