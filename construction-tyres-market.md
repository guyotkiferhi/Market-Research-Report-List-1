<p><ul><li>Bias Ply</li><li>Radial Ply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction tires market is primarily categorized into bias ply and radial ply types. </p><p>Bias ply tires consist of layers of fabric that crisscross at an angle, providing flexibility and durability but resulting in less stability at higher speeds. </p><p>In contrast, radial ply tires feature layers arranged perpendicularly, enhancing tread and sidewall flexibility, offering better fuel efficiency and improved traction. </p><p>These differences impact performance, longevity, and cost, influencing choice based on specific construction equipment needs and operational conditions.</p></p>
